Particle Theory of Matter

The five main ideas of the particle theory of matter are:

  1. All matter is made up of tiny particles that have empty spaces between them.
  2. Different substances are made up of different kinds of particles.
  3. The particles of a substance move faster as its temperature increases. (Solubility increases with the rise in temperature)
  4. Particles attract each other.
  5. Particles are in constant random motion.
