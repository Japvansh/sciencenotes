Mixtures

There are 2 types of mixtures; Heterogeneous & Homogeneous

"Hetero" means different and "Homo" means the same."

Heterogeneous: Consists of visibly different substances or phases (solid, liquid, gas). They can be separated by filtering. Separate layers in the substance. They are mixed mechanically.

Homogeneous:  has the same uniform appearance and composition throughout;  maintains one phase (solid, liquid, gas). Commonly referred to as solutions. They are mixed chemically.

E.g. Air is a mixture because it is made up of elements, compounds, and molecules
