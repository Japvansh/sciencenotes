Substances

Pure Substances are substances that cannot be broken down into smaller substances and are made up of similar atoms, or elements.

Elements are substances that are only made up of one type of atom that cannot be broken down into different substances.

Compound substances are substances that are made up of 2 or more elements.

Molecules are small components of substances

Mixtures are made up of elements, compounds, and molecules.



Suspension: Particle size = big (visible)

Solution: Particle size = small (not visible)

Colloids: Submerged particles, it is a foamy or leathery substance (Cheese, shaving cream, etc).

Particles are not visible but are in-between particles in suspension and solution





Solution - the mixture formed when a substance dissolves in it

Solute - the substance that dissolves

Solvent - the liquid in the solution

Dissolve - mixing of a substance in a liquid

Soluble - a substance which can dissolve (mix in a liquid)

Insoluble - a substance which cannot dissolve (mix in a liquid)
